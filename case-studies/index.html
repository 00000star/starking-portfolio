Safety-first MVP

Clean Water Field Guide

A conservative offline emergency water guide that uses citations, safety boundaries, and print mode instead of pretending uncertainty does not exist.

  • Risk checks, boil guidance, bleach dose helper, and storage checklist.
  • Uses CDC, EPA, and WHO guidance as explicit source anchors.
  • Shows how to design around public-health trust burden.

Disaster Mesh Communications

A local-first coordination PWA for community incidents, supply tracking, task assignment, and radio plans when normal digital workflows break down.

  • IndexedDB storage, JSON export/import, and print handoff.
  • Designed around stale data, safety notes, and low-trust conditions.
  • Focused on small groups that currently coordinate with chat and paper.

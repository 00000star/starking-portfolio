# Open Medical Translation Cards

## Mission

Build an offline card generator for communicating basic medical facts across language barriers during travel, clinics, shelters, disasters, and emergencies.

## Problem

People may need to communicate allergies, medications, chronic conditions, symptoms, emergency contacts, pregnancy status, disability needs, or consent preferences when they do not share a language with helpers.

## MVP

- Medical profile builder.
- Allergy and medication card.
- Symptom phrase cards.
- Emergency contact card.
- Printable wallet cards.
- QR card that opens an offline HTML profile.
- Translation status labels.

## Technical Plan

- Static PWA.
- Local-only storage.
- Print stylesheet.
- Translation JSON packs.
- Review metadata for each phrase.

## Safety Rules

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not recommend treatment.
- Mark translations as reviewed, machine-translated, or unreviewed.
- Make it clear that the card supports communication but does not replace medical care.

## Portfolio Value

This project demonstrates health-adjacent caution, accessibility, localization, print UX, local-only privacy, and disciplined safety copy.

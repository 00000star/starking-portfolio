## What Donations Support

## Funding Principles

- Keep core public-good tools open where possible.
- Be clear about what is built, planned, and experimental.
- Avoid overclaiming in health, legal, safety, or disaster-response tools.
- Treat donations as support for development, not a guarantee of outcomes.
